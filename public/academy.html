OUR POPULAR COURSES

Introduction to web development

Web development is the process of building websites and applications for the internet, or for a private network known as an intranet. Web development is not concerned with the design of a website; rather, it's all about the coding and programming that powers the website's functionality.

Introduction to UI/UX design

User Experience is the entire makeup of a product spanning from problem identification, research, ideation, prototype, and usability testing. The visual aspect of UX is referred to as UI ( User Interface) The importance of UX and UI is that it increases user's interaction with a product whilst satisfying the user.

Introduction to Photography

Photography is the process of recording visual images by capturing light rays on a light-sensitive recording medium. In layman's terms, photography is simply the process of capturing light with a camera to create an image.

Introduction to graphics design

Offers an overview of what visual design is as a means of communication, graphics design and the large professional opportunities in today's world. Students will get enlightened on various iconic designs, the concepts behind them and great designers, both past and present. Online platforms which help to improve one's designing skills.
