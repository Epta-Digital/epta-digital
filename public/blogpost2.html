8 Digital Skills You Should Learn

Esene Osemudiame Goodness

Dec 7Â·2 mins read

Being a Job seeker or an Entrepreneur without a digital skill is you saying "Don't mind me, I am not ready". With the continuous evolution of technology, digital skills has become one of the most sought after.
Below are 8 Digital skills you should learn:

  1. Digital Marketing: In simple words, Digital marketing is marketing done through digital technologies such as mobile phones, laptops, televisions etc. It is easier, stress-free and reaches a wide range of people. Digital marketing varies in different forms such as Email marketing, Social media marketing, Search Engine Optimization and much more.

  2. Copy and Content writing: This is writing to persuade or encourage an audience to carry out an action. It is required for any kind of business idea and one of the underestimated skill for a business idea.

  3. Videography and Photography: This is the act of capturing moments for individuals and companies.

  4. Web and App Development: It is the process and practice of developing websites and applications.

  5. Data Analytics: is the process of Inspecting, Transforming and Modelling data to discover useful information to make conclusions and support decision making.

  6. Cyber security: Cyber security is defending computers, servers, mobile devices, electronic systems, networks and data from malicious attacks.

  7. Digital Project Management: It is a streamlined process of managing online projects from conception to completion, within budget and using a certain amount of resources. It involves planning, tracking, delegating, reviewing and measuring results usually done with a project management software.

  8. Programming: This is designing and building of a set of codes or program to carryout a specific task

Very few Businesses know how to defend and insulate itself from a cyber war that spells economic ruin, so they tend to employ or partner with digital skilled personnel.
