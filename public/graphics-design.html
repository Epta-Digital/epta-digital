Introduction to Graphics Design

What you will learn

Principles of Graphics design

The basics of graphics design is explored here, the elements, principles and how they can be combined in practice. Students learn the language of visual designs, enhance their decisions as a designer and get to know the software.

Intro to colours/getting creative

White and black, Navy and teal, Pink and raisim. Color combination is a critical part of a good design. Exploring this world, we take you on a journey through the elements and principle of colors, theory and tools needed for a great color design, choice of color and COLOR TALK.

Utilizing typography/equipping your toolbox

Typography plays an integral role in graphic designs. Get introduced to what typography is and it's effects on graphic design, theory of typography and typography in practice with in-depth knowledge of Typographic designs.

How you will learn

Access to both virtual and physical classes

Our course program affords you the flexibility of choosing a learning method that best suits you(virtual or physical)

Hands-on project based learning

During the course of your training, you will work on practical projects to help you achieve mastery in your chosen field

Access to a wide range of community support

Our team of experienced instructors will be with you all the way to help give you a smooth learning experience.
