Gen Zed

Esene Osemudiame Goodness

Dec 7Â·2 mins read

You are probably wondering what Gen Zed is, well you are about to get your mind blown. When I first heard about Gen Zed, I thought it was an action movie. Funny right? Actually, Gen Zed is the acronym of Generation Z. Gen Zed are the successors of the Millennial and the predecessors of Generation Alpha. They are born between the year 1995 to 2010. We are basically the youths, the "Soro soke" generation.

Gen Zed are more diverse than any other previous generation ethnically and racially. Though they tend to very much look like millennials in political and social issues, they are more progressive and pro-government. Taking the United States as an example, Gen Zeds are less likely to see the US as superior to other nations. During the just concluded US election, majority of Gen Zeds voted against Donald Trump and voted for Joe Biden because they find growing racial and ethnic diversity as a good thing.

Presently, they are also the most educated and technology driven generation (digital natives), having little or no memory of the world before smartphones and technology. They might also the the most cultured generation ever. Gen Zeds are decision makers and prepare themselves for varieties of problems. They are not stereotyped, rather they shape their individual identities to any situation they find themselves just like the chameleon.

Most companies are attracted to the Generation Zed not only because they are technology driven but also because they are mostly influencers. Technology has also given them a very high degree of connectivity among themselves which speeds up technological trends as well. For companies, this bring challenges but mostly brings attractive opportunities.

This is the era of Gen Zed, so when you see someone of the age range, fist bump them and say "What's up Z".
