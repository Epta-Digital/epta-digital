Introduction to Photography

What you will learn

The concept of Photography

Photography is the process of recording visual images by capturing light rays on a light-sensitive recording medium. In layman's terms, photography is simply the process of capturing light with a camera to create an image.

Understanding Composition/mastering lighting

The composition of an image is simply the way all the individual subjects within our frame combine to form the final image. The commonly known composition techniques are;

  • Rule of thirds
  • Foreground interest and depth.
  • Leading lines
  • Rule of odds
  • Patterns and textures

Understanding Focal length

Focal length, usually represented in milimiters (mm), is the basic description of a photographic lens. The logner the focal length, the narrower the angle of the view and the higher the magnification. The shorter the focal length, the wider the angle of view and the lower the magnification

Post production

Post production usually involves applying a certain range of image editing operations to the raw image format. This is a range of proprietary and free and open-source software running on a range of operating systems available to do this work
