Introduction to Photography

What you will learn

The concept of Photography

Photography is the process of recording visual images by capturing light rays on a light-sensitive recording medium. In layman's terms, photography is simply the process of capturing light with a camera to create an image.

Understanding Composition/mastering lighting

The composition of an image is simply the way all the individual subjects within our frame combine to form the final image. The commonly known composition techniques are; 1.Rule of thirds 2.Foreground interest and depth. 3. Leading lines 4. Rule of odds. 5.Patterns and textures.
